Formal syntax formatting sampler

Different ways of formatting and displaying the R7RS formal syntax.

Marked-up text

One paragraph per production

⟨token⟩ → ⟨identifier⟩ | ⟨boolean⟩ | ⟨number⟩ | ⟨character⟩ | ⟨string⟩ | ( | ) | #( | #u8 | ' | | ` | , | ,@ | .

⟨delimiter⟩ → ⟨whitespace⟩ | ⟨vertical line⟩ | ( | ) | " | ;

⟨intraline whitespace⟩ → ⟨space or tab⟩

⟨whitespace⟩ → ⟨intraline whitespace⟩ | ⟨line ending⟩

⟨vertical line⟩ → |

⟨line ending⟩ → ⟨newline⟩ | ⟨return⟩ ⟨newline⟩ | ⟨return⟩

⟨comment⟩ → ; ⟨all subsequent characters up to a line ending⟩ | ⟨nested comment⟩ | #; ⟨intertoken space⟩ ⟨datum⟩

⟨nested comment⟩ → #| ⟨comment text⟩ ⟨comment cont⟩* |#

⟨comment text⟩ → ⟨character sequence not containing #| or |#⟩

⟨comment cont⟩ → ⟨nested comment⟩ ⟨comment text⟩

⟨directive⟩ → #!fold-case | #!no-fold-case

Plain-text EBNF transcription

<token> --> <identifier> | <boolean> | <number> | <character> | <string>
    | ( | ) | #( | #u8( | ' | ` | , | , | .

<delimiter> --> <whitespace> | <vertical line> | ( | )
    | " | ;

<intraline whitespace> --> <space or tab>

<whitespace> --> <intraline whitespace> | <line ending>

<vertical line> --> [a pipe character, U+007C]

<line ending> --> <newline> | <return> <newline> | <return>

<comment> --> ; <all subsequent characters up to a line ending>
    | <nested comment>
    | #; <intertoken space> <datum>

<nested comment> --> #| <comment text>
                        <comment cont>* |#

<comment text> --> <character sequence not containing #| or |#>

<comment cont> --> <nested comment> <comment text>

<directive> --> #!fold-case | #!no-fold-case
